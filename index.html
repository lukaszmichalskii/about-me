Lukasz Michalski

I'm a research student at CERN in Geneva, where I work on real-time and quasi real-time data acquisition and physics algorithms with heterogeneous computing at the CMS experiment. My research focuses on jet clustering & tagging, exploring rare physics cases and optimization for high-speed AI inference for Next Generation Trigger project.

At Intel R&D, I worked on GPU Software Development (AI/CV) in AI Graphics Software for DirectX 3D team. At Nokia, I contributed to internal tools for analyzing eNB machine logs and developed an in-house source code management system for SoC hardware solutions. Currently, I am pursuing an MSc in artificial intelligence at Wroclaw University of Science and Technology.

Research
